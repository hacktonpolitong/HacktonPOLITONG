# Demo Video Script

Total target duration: 60 to 90 seconds.

## Screen 1 — Start Screen (5-10 sec)

Spoken line: "PilotOps AI helps a Chinese warehouse automation vendor turn one product into a first Italian pilot strategy, not a generic market report."

What to click: click `Open Demo Flow` or `Start Analysis`.

## Screen 2 — Product Intake (10-15 sec)

Spoken line: "The intake is prefilled with a fictional Chinese AMR vendor. For demo stability, the AMR/3PL scenario stays locked, while the presenter can rename the vendor."

What to show or edit: show `Company name`, `Product category`, `Target market`, `Documentation status`, `Product description`, `Proof available`, and `Known constraints`. Click `Run Pilot Analysis`.

## Screen 3 — Building the Pilot Control Room (5-10 sec)

Spoken line: "The analysis route builds the control room from the demo profile and local seed data, with optional live AI only when server-side OpenRouter keys are configured."

What to highlight: `Parsing product profile`, `Matching Italian warehouse segments`, `Selecting pilot workflow`, `Ranking target account shortlist`, `Identifying buyer trust gaps`, `Generating pilot offer`, and `Preparing sales pack`.

## Screen 4 — Pilot Control Room — First Viewport (15-20 sec)

Spoken line: "The first viewport gives the expansion team the decision core: pilot fit score, best first buyer segment, and the warehouse process to pilot first."

What to highlight: `Pilot Fit Score`, `Best First Buyer Segment`, `Best Warehouse Process`, and `Why Now`.

## Screen 5 — Pilot Control Room — Trust Gaps + Target Account Shortlist (15-20 sec)

Spoken line: "Instead of scraped leads, the shortlist is curated company-level Italian logistics data ranked against the selected segment and internal-transport pilot fit."

What to highlight: `Trust Gap Analysis`, `Recommended Pilot Offer`, and `Target Account Shortlist`. Point out company name, official website, region, category, warehouse signals, process fit, buyer roles, outreach angle, and source note.

## Screen 6 — Pilot Control Room — Sales Pack + Next Actions (10-15 sec)

Spoken line: "The final sections turn the recommendation into sales execution: objections, proof checklist, ready-to-send sales assets, and a seven-day action plan."

What to highlight: `Buyer Objection Battlecard`, `Documentation Checklist`, `Sales Pack`, and `Next 7 Days Action Plan`.

Closing line: "Not a market report — a first-pilot package, ready to send."
